Online Voting Instructions for Indian Constituency

Voting is the cornerstone of democracy. Follow these steps to cast your vote online securely.

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must be a registered voter.
  • You must have a valid Voter ID issued by the Election Commission of India (ECI).
  • Your Aadhaar card must be linked with your Voter ID (if required).
  • Secure internet access is required.

Step-by-Step Online Voting Process

1. Registration for Online Voting

  • Visit the official Election Commission of India website.
  • Click on "Online Voting Registration" (if available).
  • Log in using your registered mobile number and OTP.
  • Complete the online voting request form and submit required documents.

2. Login to the Online Voting Portal

  • On election day, visit the official Online Voting Portal.
  • Log in using your Voter ID and OTP authentication.

3. Verify Your Identity

  • Complete biometric or Aadhaar-based authentication (if applicable).
  • Use a secure device for voting.

4. Select Your Constituency & Candidate

  • Select your constituency.
  • View the candidate list and choose your preferred candidate.

5. Review & Submit Your Vote

  • Double-check your selection before submitting.
  • Click "Submit Vote" to confirm.
  • You will receive a confirmation message and reference number.

Important Guidelines

  • Do not share your login details or OTP with anyone.
  • Use a secure and private network for voting.
  • Once submitted, your vote cannot be changed.
