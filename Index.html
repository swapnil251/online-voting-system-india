Voter Registration & Verification

Citizens must register on the official election portal and verify their identity using Aadhaar, voter ID, or other credentials.

2

Secure Login & Authentication

Voters log in using a one-time password (OTP), biometric authentication, or other secure methods to prevent fraud.

3

Casting the Vote

After successful authentication, voters select their preferred candidate/party on a digital ballot and confirm their choice.

4

Vote Submission & Confirmation

The vote is encrypted, submitted securely, and the voter receives a confirmation (or receipt) ensuring their vote has been recorded.
